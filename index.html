The Electric Guitar Pickup

A guitar pickup is the first element that comes into making an electic guitar have it's sound.
The pickup "picks up" the vibration of the guitar strings and converts the
mechanical energy of the vibrating strings into electrical energy that goes into an amplifier.
The electric guitar pickup is can be on any electric stringed instrument.
Examples include guitars, basses, mandolins, banjos, pianos, violins, and more.
