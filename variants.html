Single-Coil Pickup

Identified by their single set of poles and single coil of winding.

Humbucker Pickup

Identified by their double set of poles and double coil of winding.

P-90 Pickup

Identified by their single set of poles and double coil of winding.

Active Pickup

Identified by their use of an external power source.
